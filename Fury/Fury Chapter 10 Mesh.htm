Mark knew that sequestration was something Ichigo was passionate about, and he knew Ichigo had something to do with the Mars Uprising.

He drilled down into Varija’s personal details

 

Varija Aletea Kunasada born 23rd Jan 2272 to parents Mary Jane Crawford and Roger Kunasada.

 

Diagnosed as gifted at an early age she was fast-tracked academically, graduating high school at age 13 and entering Yarra Polytechnic at age 14. It was there that she befriended Selina Seager, leader of hacktivist group ‘Students For Open Government’. Despite the 4 year age gap she became involved with the older girl. She completed her four year degree in tarka engineering in two years and was accepted into Yarra Polytechnics Doctorate Program at age 16 with a proposed thesis on the practical application of tarka for the cure of Mental Disorders.

 

On 13 February 2288 Selina Seager disappeared under mysterious circumstances. The 2nd in command of SFOG Trevor Luebke stepped aside in favour of Varija, endorsing her as the new leader. Despite her youth she proved a far more effective leader for the SFOG than her lover, exposing a series of damaging revelations about members of Michael Palmer’s Cabinet. Matters escalated when her parents were killed when a malfunctioning fuel tanker crashed into the family home. Varija insisted that OSIO and the Palmer government where behind both Selina Seager’s disappearance and her parents death (in 2291 rogue OSIO agents Abigail Brzoza and Brannon Figueredo would be convicted of her parents murder. However no evidence has been unearthed linking Selina Seager’s disappearance to any of Oceania’s governmental agencies).

 

Varija quit her studies and went on a crusade that exposed every shady undertaking, every backroom deal brokered by Michael Palmer’s government and the ‘Free Trade’ Party. Civil unrest and mass party defections triggered a constitutional crisis forcing an early election. The ‘Free Trade’ Party was decimated in the 2289 elections and was never again a force in Oceanian politics.

 

Varija then turned her attention to Oceanian Intelligence agency OSIO. Despite having a good reputation as being hardened against cyber-incursion OSIO turned out to be as vulnerable to Varija’s talents as the Palmer government had been. A series of damaging revelations ensued, culminating in a series of emails that implicated agents Abigail Brzoza and Brannon Figueredo in her parent’s death. Shortly after this, maintenance technician Otto Herrick confessed to helping the agents sabotage the fuel tanker. The agents went into hiding.  However they were arrested in hospital after the vehicle in which they were traveling suffered a malfunction similar to the one that caused Varija’s parents’ deaths.

 

 

The Intelligence community struck back, issuing warrants and demanding that Varija be called to account for her illegal cyber-incursion activities. However Varija had been in hiding since the failed 2288 assassination attempt and only a core cadre of key SFOG members knew how to contact Varija. The OSIO tried to find Varija by applying pressure to the SFOG however this caused many of the core members of SFOG to go into hiding and for Varija to end her association with SFOG.

 

Over the next few years Varija claimed responsibility for exposing many damaging secrets of powerful organizations in both the private and public sectors. She then fell silent in 2291.

 

There was then a period where every new talented cyber-incursion specialist whether male or female was accused of being Varija in disguise. Carine Ford of ‘Free Mind’ and the ‘Anti-Sequestration League’ fame and Thomas Helrich, Head of Security for Free Society International, being two of the most famous examples.

 

Varija's status in the security community was recognized at Defcon 2293 by the Gray Hats with her election to the office of Mage as ‘The Fury’. At the age of 21 one of the youngest security specialists to ever receive this honor. Her status was also recognized in a different way with InterSec’s inclusion of her in their Most Wanted list.

 

After evidence (disputed) of her involvement with two famous jailbreaks (that of Jason Hammond and that of fellow Mage ‘The Raptor’) she achieved the number 3 spot in InterSec’s Most Wanted list edging out Ivan Matveev, the butcher of Omsk, who was bumped to 4th place. Her association with ‘The Raptor’ also led InterSec to include her in their known terrorist list as ‘The Raptor’ had been included on the InterSec known terrorist list after his support for the controversial Nwosu regime.

 

After her listing as a known terrorist OSIO pushed for and achieved an 'Extremely Dangerous - Full Force Authorized' listing for their old nemesis at InterSec. The only InterSec member state who currently disputes Varija’s listing as a known terrorist and her ED-FFA status is Israel. Israel’s uncharacteristic defiance of their own allies on Varija behalf has led many to speculate that rumors that Varija had worked as a foreign instructor at their notorious ‘The Academy’ and had provided overwatch for the Pwahiwa hostage rescue were in fact true.

 

After 2294 Varija disappeared. However the similarity in name to Martian Senator Var Kunasada has led to speculation that the Senator is Varija and that she has been on Mars since 2294 and was involved in the Mars Uprising of 2298 in some manner. The difficulty in obtaining information from Mars has prevented any verification of these speculations.

 

 

Mark wondered how much of the article was true. He wondered if she would actually answer his questions if he asked her how much of it was true.

 

The article has a less than flattering 3D picture of Varija at age 16 looking like a female version of Darth Maul with giant bats wings. She had an angry expression on her face which was redundant as the entire body was a statement of anger.

 

He sighed. <At least he had a name.> he thought. He opened a history tutorial and set about trying to repair a 300 year gap in his knowledge.
